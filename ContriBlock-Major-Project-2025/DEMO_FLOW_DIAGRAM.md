1. **Landing** вҶ’ Show platform
2. **Sign Up** вҶ’ Create account
3. **KYC** вҶ’ Verify identity
4. **Admin Review** вҶ’ Approve KYC
5. **Submit** вҶ’ Upload contribution
6. **Admin Review** вҶ’ Approve contribution
7. **IPFS** вҶ’ File uploaded
8. **Blockchain** вҶ’ Hash recorded
9. **Rewards** вҶ’ Tokens credited
10. **Marketplace** вҶ’ Browse & purchase
11. **Dashboard** вҶ’ View analytics
